Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Your Privacy

Puzzle Memories is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number or precise location.

Anonymous Analytics

To improve the game experience, Puzzle Memories may collect anonymous usage statistics. These statistics cannot be used to identify you personally.

  • Total play time
  • Completed puzzles
  • Challenge progression
  • Game performance and crash reports
  • General gameplay statistics

Purchases

If you purchase premium content or subscriptions, all payment processing is handled securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties. Anonymous analytics may be processed by trusted service providers solely to improve the application.

Children

Puzzle Memories is not intended to collect personal information from children.
